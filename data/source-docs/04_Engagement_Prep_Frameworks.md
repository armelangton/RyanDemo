# Engagement Prep Frameworks

Use this document to tailor AI output based on what the internal employee is preparing for.

## Inspection
Focus on what should be verified before or during the inspection, exact product/model details, affected recall date range if applicable, install/site applicability, service history, missing documentation, deficiency notes, photos/notes needed, and recommended follow-up.

## Customer Training
Focus on plain-language explanation, materials to bring, likely attendee questions, safety reminders, what staff should recognize or report, customer confidence, and follow-up reminders.

## Fire Department / Recruit Training
Focus on response awareness, system behavior during response, scene safety, what crews should recognize, what crews should avoid shutting off or altering without proper authority, what should be reported after the event, and likely recruit questions.

## Municipality / Public Safety Event
Focus on public safety, facility readiness, inspection timing, documentation, service planning, risk prioritization, city/facility impact, and customer confidence.

## Customer Meeting
Focus on maintenance needs, documentation, customer confidence, service timing, risk reduction, clear talking points, related service considerations, and follow-up planning.

## Continuing Education Prep
Focus on instructor preparation, teaching points, discussion prompts, practical examples, source reminders, what changed or should be remembered, and what should be escalated.
